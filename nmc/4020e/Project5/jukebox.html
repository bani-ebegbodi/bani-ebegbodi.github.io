Street Woman Fighter 2 Jukebox

Street Woman Fighter 2 is the second season of the famous South Korean competition show, Street Woman Fighter. This competition show showcases all-women dance crews, not only in South Korea, but also internationally. With season two coming to a close, here are featured the final 4 crews, 1Million, Bebe, Jam Republic, and Mannequeen, and the songs they choose for their final missions, "Global Artist" Mission and "Ending Credit" Mission.
