The dance crew Bebe, one of the finalists and the winner for Street Woman Fighter Season 2, is the youngest finalist group. Many of their dances have gained popularity and set trends since the release of the season. For their Global Mission Performance, they choose Rihanna and wrapped up the season with a masked performance as their Ending Credit Performance.
