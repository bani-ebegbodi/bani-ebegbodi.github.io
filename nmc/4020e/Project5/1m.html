The dance crew 1MILLION, one of the finalists for Street Woman Fighter Season 2, is well-recognized within the dance community, particularly for their significant online presence created through various choreography videos on YouTube. During the show, their Mega Crew Mission performance stood out the most, contributing to the 10 million views it garnered on YouTube. For their Global Mission Performance, they choose Ariana Grande and wrapped up the season with Imagine Dragons as their Ending Credit Performance.
